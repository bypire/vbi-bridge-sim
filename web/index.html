Vehicle–Bridge Interaction

A quarter-car crosses a simply-supported beam. Deflection is magnified — (real peak —); colour shows bending moment.

Method: Euler–Bernoulli beam FEM (consistent M, K) coupled to a 2-DOF quarter-car, RK4 time integration. Verified against the Frýba moving-force closed form (V2) and analytical references (V1). The axle weight is recovered from the bridge response by Moses' B-WIM algorithm.

Honest limits: single span, linear, smooth road (no roughness), one axle. Displacements are tiny in reality — hence the magnification factor, shown above so nothing is faked.
